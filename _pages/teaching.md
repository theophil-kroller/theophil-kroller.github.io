## Teaching Approach

My teaching is application-oriented, dialogical, and practice-based. I aim to help students not only understand concepts, but also apply them in realistic decision-making situations.

A central goal of my teaching is to make complex topics accessible without oversimplifying them. I work with case studies, role-based exercises, group puzzles, startup examples, business plan development, and reflective discussions.

## Teaching Areas

- Entrepreneurship
- Business Plan Development
- Design Thinking
- Marketing for Entrepreneurship
- General Management and Organization
- Strategic Management
- Leadership
- Organizational Design

## Selected Teaching Formats

- Case-based learning
- Role-card exercises
- Group puzzles
- Startup simulations
- Business plan coaching
- Reflective discussions
- AI-supported learning experiments

## Teaching Development

I continuously develop my teaching through peer feedback, didactic reflection, course redesign, and formal teaching development activities.
